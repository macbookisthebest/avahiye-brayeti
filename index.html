About us:

Located in the heart of Zakho, between two rivers and right along the bustling high street, this newly constructed building blends modern functionality with classic architectural charm. Built with traditional bricks that echo timeless design, the structure features five well-positioned shops on the ground floor , making it a vibrant part of the local mall scene. Above, a spacious hall provides room for gatherings or events, while a cozy café crowns the top floor, offering scenic views and a comfy atmosphere
